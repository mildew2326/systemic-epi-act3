Act 3 · Diabetes
Mechanism

Gene programs change insulin response.

What shifts

Inflammatory signaling alters how effectively insulin-related machinery works on and inside cells.

What you get

Poorer response to insulin. Weaker glucose handling. Inflammation-associated insulin resistance.

Tissue-dependent biology. Not “IL-6 alone = diabetes.” Not “perio alone caused diabetes.”
